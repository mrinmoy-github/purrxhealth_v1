What is CBD ?

First, we must recognize the human body and its endocannabinoid system (ECS). The ECS is responsible for controlling the brain, endocrine, nervous and immune systems. It has a regulatory effect and response to stress, secretion of hormones and overall homeostasis. The ECS maintains our health by creating balance within the body.

When the ECS becomes unbalanced, the systems in which IT controls will begin to dysfunction. This leads to ailments within the body. The body seeks to regulate itself again and find that healthy EQUILIBRIUM.
